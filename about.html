Favorite Books

I am not an avid reader, most of the books I do read are IT, business process or Six Sigma related, not really exciting. However, there are four books that have had a deep impact on my life and how I try to live my life. They are:

  • Man's Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl
  • The Tao of Pooh by Benjamin Hoff
  • The Dance of Anger by Harriet Lerner
  • The Five Dysfunctions of a Team by Patrick Lenciconi
